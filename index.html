Cape Town Informal Settlement Map

Our Constitution guarantees all residents in Cape Town the right to adequate housing and access to water, basic sanitation as well as dignity, safety and security. There are around 437 informal settlements in Cape Town ranging from 3 homes to nearly 8000 homes. This map helps you to find out on what land they are located, how old they are, what basic services are currently being provided and what constraints currently preventing them being upgraded.

The data used to generate this map was obtained from the City of Cape Town in 2015. Some information was obtained from sources and others through access to information requests.
